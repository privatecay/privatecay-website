About

It's no coincidence we are known as Private Cay (pronounced private key). If you are familiar with cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum and Bitshares, you know that your private key is your only means of accessing your funds. Since there are no third parties (banks) to recover your funds, understanding how to secure your private keys is crucial to your long term success with crypto.

Our logo, the X, marks your personal treasure. It's empowering and daunting all at once. Cryptocurrencies are bringing back peer to peer interactions. When you hand someone cash for goods or services, there is no middleman. It's you and the other party. Sadly, it has become almost impossible to conduct a peer to peer transaction with larger sums of money. Fees and ineffiencies in the banking system have created a broken and invasive system.

The primary purpose of this site is to provide easy to understand tutorals to help new crypto investors safely get involved. The support link at the top has well thought out articles to help you begin buying Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum and Bitcoin Cash. We also have articles to help you understand the blogging platform called Steemit.com and decentralized exchange called Bitshares. We hope you enjoy your experience with PrivateCay and hope we can assist you as you learn about this new world.
